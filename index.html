About Me

I'm 17 years old, a High School Senior

My name is Cameron Ythan King. I attend school at Little Red Schoolhouse and Elisabeth Irwin High School (LREI), located in SoHo, New York. I am an A- student and a Prep for Prep and All Star Code member. I live in Brooklyn, New York. I consider myself an athelte, a musician, a comedian, an artist, a designer and I am an aspiring Computer Engineer. In addition to playing three Varisty Sports( Soccer, Basketball, and Track and Field) and playing four instruments ( Piano, Drums, Trumpet, and Baritone Horn), I paint and I code. For example, I made this website on my own. I recently represented LREI at the White Privilege Conference in Louisville, Kentucky.

What I do?.

Play Music

I play four instruments and I rap and make beats in GarageBand and Logic Pro. I play Piano, Drums, Trumpet, and Baritone Horn. I lead the trumpet section of the Jazz Band, and low brass section of the Wind Ensemble.

Play Sports

I play Basketball, Soccer, and Track and Field. I was JV Captain of my school's basketball team in 2013, and now I am the starting Center for Varisty Basketball (2014-2015).
